About TorchEBM

TorchEBM is a PyTorch library for energy-based modeling, with support for flow and diffusion methods. It provides composable tools for defining energy landscapes, training with various objectives (flow/equilibrium matching, contrastive divergence, score matching), and sampling via MCMC, optimization, or learned continuous-time dynamics (flow-based).

Created and maintained by Soran Ghaderi, Incoming computer science DPhil, currently Research Engineer at CoSTAR National Lab, and open-source ML developer.
